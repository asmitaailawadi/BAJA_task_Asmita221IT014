About us

We also go by the name BNR.We're a passionate community of engineering enthusiasts formed in 2007, driven by innovation and excellence. Our relentless journey pushes automotive design boundaries, crafting cutting-edge off-road vehicles for Baja SAE dominance. With a rich history of success, we're at the forefront of technological innovation in motorsports. At NITK Baja Racing, we cultivate dreams and inspire the next generation of engineers. Beyond competition, our aim is to foster creativity, teamwork, and continuous improvement, empowering future automotive enthusiasts and industry leaders through hands-on experience and real-world engineering principles.
